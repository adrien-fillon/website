---
title: How can a leader effectively convey his vision ?
date: 2021-01-29
---

Current research shows that leaders generally convey an abstract vision made up of sentences that are impossible to visualize.
An effective vision must be visualizable.
Thus, the best visions are made from simple and concrete visual metaphors
